Guiding Objectives
1. Find the free ligand concentration that max RU reached starts to plateau
2. Generate max RU reached value at  low free ligand concentration
3. For a single data set, repeat 6 times  to allow calculation of standard  deviation
4. Produce a Langmuir plot of max RU  reached against free ligand  concentration
5. Derive Kd from Langmuir plot being the  free ligand concentration at half  maximum max RU reached
6. From the sensorgram disassociation curve, calculate koff using the first order disassociation rate equation
7. Using Kd and koff calculated, derive  kon
